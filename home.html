The importance of having an anti-squatting door in Spain today

Squatting came to Spain in the 2008 crisis to stay and it has shot up to disturbing figures, nearly 100,000 establishments have been squatted throughout Spain. The most affected localities are Madrid, which is estimated to have 25,000 squatted homes, only behind Catalonia where it’s estimated that there’re 30,000. A report reveals that squatting costs the owners of these cities 21.4 million euros every year. The analysis puts on the table the losses faced by the owners of the flats in Madrid and Barcelona, reaching 11.9 million in the case of the first of these cities and 9.8 in the second. Furthermore, we can’t ignore the communities of Castilla La Mancha and Andalusia, although the situation is repeated throughout Spain and far from improving in recent years, it’s increasing.

Because currently there’s still a legal vacuum that leaves homeowners who are squatted in absolute defenselessness and considering the slowness of justice and legal procedures cause a very specific situation. Homeowners who take over mortgage, electricity and water bills are left to their own devices while squatters enjoy these benefits.

An anti-squat door is the best guarantee of security for any home, office or second residence. And don’t live in fear of a possible squatting of your home. In summary, the eviction of squatters is a rather long and expensive process, and the main measures that are being carried out are aimed at preventing them from entering and the most efficient and competent way is by installing an Anti-squat door.

How are our Anti-Squat Doors Made?

Our Anti-squat Doors have a reinforced metallic steel structure with an approximate weight of 75kg of the complete structure.

Multi-point lock and high security cylinder with triple antibumping protection. An innovative system with double pin and double spring, made of stainless steel and attached to the cylinder body by 4 stainless steel pins. Maximum anti-drill protection with bars on the rotor and the body, shell in the lower area and additional steel pins. Protective shield made of thick solid steel core. Anti-extraction, anti-drilling and antijaw assembly. Trim made of solid steel, offering greater protection and security and preventing opening both on the outside and inside.

Antiokupa Gate Installation

An anti-squat door is easily installed in approximately 15 to 45 minutes, in front of the original door of a house and without the need for work. This is installed on the door frame that is to be protected to avoid the forced entry of a squatter, without the original door being damaged or altered, since they open outwards. They’re mounted with Fischer chemical anchors and Hilti tamper-proof screws. In addition, the antisquat doors are easily disassembled and can be reused in other homes.
